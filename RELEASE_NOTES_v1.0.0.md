# Distant Lights v1.0.0

## Highlights
- First public release of **Distant Lights**, a physics-informed light-to-sound lab.
- Interactive exploration of three model families:
  - electrical / mechanical hum
  - photoacoustic sound generation
  - sonification of sub-audio light modulation
- Browser-based audio synthesis and preset workflow.
- Research-oriented framing for comparing different mechanisms behind “hearing lights”.
